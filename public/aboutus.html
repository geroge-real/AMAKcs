Welcome to AMAK

Here at AMAK (Aklatan Mo: Aklatan Ko), we aim to form a new generation of writers and bibliophiles by giving them access to new books daily and delving further into the beauty of literature. This webpage allows users to see the history, founders, values, mission, and the services offered for AMAK.

Founded by George Froyalde and Rey Adriano, AMAK is governed by the values of Creativity, Accessibility, and Community. We believe in fostering a passion for words and making literature accessible to the modern person. This webpage also provides information on the significant milestones, achievements, statistics, target audience, contact information, and future plans of our website. These features would show the overall success and build trust with our users.
